# How to Install as App (PWA)

## On Android (Chrome):
1. Open this link in Chrome: https://mjb199523.github.io/Electricity-bill/
2. Tap the **three dots** (menu) in top right
3. Tap **"Add to Home Screen"** or **"Install App"**
4. Tap **"Install"**
5. The app will appear in your app drawer like a regular app

## On iPhone (Safari):
1. Open the link in Safari
2. Tap the **share button** (square with arrow)
3. Scroll down and tap **"Add to Home Screen"**
4. Tap **"Add"**

## What you get:
- App icon on home screen
- Opens like a native app (no browser address bar)
- Works offline (after first visit)
- Full screen experience
